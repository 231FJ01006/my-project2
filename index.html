About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, born February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, is one of the greatest footballers of all time. Known for his unmatched work ethic, physicality, and goal-scoring abilities, Ronaldo has played for top clubs such as Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al-Nassr, as well as captaining the Portugal national team.

Ronaldo has won numerous accolades, including five Ballon d'Or awards, four European Golden Shoes, and over 30 major trophies. He is the all-time leading goal scorer in the UEFA Champions League and international football.

Career Highlights

Ronaldo began his senior career with Sporting CP before moving to Manchester United in 2003. He won three Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League with United. At Real Madrid, he won four Champions League titles, became the club's all-time top scorer, and secured two La Liga titles. Ronaldo also won Serie A titles with Juventus and continues to excel in his career, setting records in every team he has played for.
